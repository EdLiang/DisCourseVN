About Us

Our Mission

DisCourse strives to create an environment for a community that encourages creativity.
Have a story to tell? Put it down on paper and create your own story. The ultimate goal for our visual novel branch is to, not just share our stories with the community, but to create a visual novel creator for the community to share their own stories with us and everyone else.
The ultimate project should be efficient, flexible and UI friendly to ensure that the writer may focus on their story and art.
